SYNOPSIS

01
Nia and Silas work in the same office.
They live in a society where human emotion has been genetically eliminated.
Everyone lives calm, violence-free, drone-like lives - until a disease breaks out that causes people to have emotions.

02
After becoming infected, Silas, now an outcast, is drawn to Nia who has managed to hide her symptoms. When they discover that they are both sick, they must decide whether to hide within their society or seek freedom elsewhere.
