현재 시각 표시

Nia and Silas work in the same office.
They live in a society where human emotion has been genetically
eliminated. Everyone lives calm, violence-free, drone-like lives - until
a disease breaks out that causes people to have emotions.

Synopsis-image
After becoming infected, Silas, now an outcast, is drawn to Nia who has
managed to hide her symptoms. When they discover that they are both
sick, they must decide whether to hide within their society or seek
freedomelsewhere.
